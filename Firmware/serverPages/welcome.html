Further notes

This Device includes following interfaces
  • SyncSerial-LED-strip may contain a buttload of LEDs
  • AsyncSerial-LED-strip may contain a buttload of LEDs
  • RGBW-LED-strip with upt to 15 LEDs if internal Boos-converter ist used
  • Each I2C-LED-expander contains 16 LEDs
Please note that following power limitations
  • Do not exceed 500 mA using the USB-plug
  • Do not exceed 2A using the power-plug
  • The RGB-Port bears maximum 200 mA in 12-V-mode
